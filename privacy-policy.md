# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 11, 2026**

Bioinformatics Learn Infinite ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website [www.bioinfolearninfinite.com](https://www.bioinfolearninfinite.com) and the Bioinfo Learn mobile application (collectively, the "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Feedback Form Submissions**: When you submit feedback through our website, we collect your name, email address, and message content.
- **Contact Information**: If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information such as your name and email address.

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage Data**: We may collect information about how you access and use the Service, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
- **Device Information**: When using our mobile app, we may collect device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

### Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small data files stored on your device. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our Service
- Respond to your feedback and inquiries
- Analyze usage patterns to improve content and user experience
- Send occasional updates about new content (only if you've opted in)

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: With trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website (e.g., hosting providers)
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law or to protect our rights

Our Service may contain links to third-party websites and resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

### Google Services

We may use Google services (such as Google Forms for feedback collection). These services are governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.
